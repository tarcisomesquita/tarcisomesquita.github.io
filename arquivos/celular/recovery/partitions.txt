/boot partition includes the kernel and the ramdisk

/system contains the entire operating system

/recovery can be considered as an alternative boot partition

/data contains the user’s data – contacts, messages, settings and apps. Wiping this partition performs a factory reset.

/cache where Android stores frequently accessed data

/misc contains miscellaneous system settings in form of on/off switches. These settings may include CID (Carrier or Region ID), USB configuration and certain hardware settings etc.
